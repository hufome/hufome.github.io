Short-cuts

Minecraft Art Tech Gallery



Controls



Movement

A - Strafe Left

S - Walk Back

W - Walk Forward

D - Strafe Right

SPACE - Jump/Fly

SHIFT- Sneak

CTRL - Sprint



Gameplay (Mouse)

Left Click - Attack/Destroy

Middle Click - Pick block

Right Click - Use Item / Place Blocks




Inventory

E - Open/Close Inventory

1 through 9 - Change hotbar slots

F - Swap item in hands

Q - Drop selected item




Multiplayer

T - Open chat

ESC - Pause/Resume

TAB - Toggle the list of players



Miscellaneous

CTRL + B - Toggles the narrator

F1 - Toggles the Heads-up display

F2 - Takes a screenshot

F5 - Toggles perspective

F11 - Toggles full-screen mode



Commands

/codame - go to the beggining of CODAME art gallery

/plots - go to the world to build your own gallery

/plot auto - automatically claim a plot

/plot trust [minecraft username] - allow someone to help build your gallery

/plot home - teleports you back to your plot

/plot visit [minecraft username] - teleports you to someone's elses gallery

examples:

/plot visit pikio - teleports you to pikio's gallery

/plot visit BurgerSenpai - teleports you to BurgerSenpai's gallery
