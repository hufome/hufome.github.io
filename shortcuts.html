Short-cuts








Movement

E - open inventory

W - Forwards, S - Backwards, A - Left, D - Right

Double space - fly








Commands

/codame - go to the beggining of CODAME art gallery

/plots - go to the world to build your own gallery

/plot auto - automatically claim a plot

/plot trust [minecraft username] - allow someone to help build your gallery

/plot home - teleports you back to your plot

/plot home - teleports you back to your plot
